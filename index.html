About Me

I work in hydrodynamic stability and computational fluid dynamics. My research focuses on instability mechanisms in rotating channel flows, porous media flows, and magnetohydrodynamic systems using spectral numerical methods and high-performance computing.

Ongoing Research

  • Non-modal transient growth analysis in rotating channel flows.
  • Instability mechanisms in anisotropic porous media flows.
  • Spectral numerical methods for hydrodynamic stability problems.

Publications

  • M. M. Hossain, M. Saha, S. Ghosh, and H. Behera, “Stability dynamics of an odd viscosity induced fluid flow over a vibrating inclined surface,” Physics of Fluids, 37(10), 104108 (2025).
  • M. Saha, S. Sengupta, S. Mukhopadhyay, and S. Ghosh, “Flow instability in a rotating channel loaded with an anisotropic porous material,” Computers & Fluids, 299, 106689 (2025).
