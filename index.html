About Me

Computational fluid dynamics researcher with experience in numerical simulation of complex flow systems using spectral collocation methods and scientific computing. Skilled in C/C++, Python, MATLAB, and HPC-based computations. Published in leading journals such as Physics of Fluids and Computers & Fluids. Interested in applying mathematical modeling and simulation techniques to engineering problems in aerospace systems and autonomous technologies.
