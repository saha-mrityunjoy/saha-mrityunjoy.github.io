About Me

I work in hydrodynamic stability and computational fluid dynamics. My research focuses on instability mechanisms in rotating channel flows, porous media flows, and magnetohydrodynamic systems using spectral numerical methods and high-performance computing.

Ongoing Research

  • Modal and non-modal stability of Poiseuille flow through anisotropic inhomogeneous porous materials.
  • Dual instability in a vertical crossflow-induced oscillating channel flow.
  • Effect of uniform vertical crossflow on rotational instability in a channel flow.
