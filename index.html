About Me

I am a computational fluid dynamics researcher specializing in hydrodynamic stability and numerical simulations of complex flow systems. My research focuses on instability mechanisms in rotating channel flows, porous media flows, and magnetohydrodynamic systems using spectral methods and high performance computing.

Research Projects

  • Hydrodynamic instability analysis of rotating channel flows loaded with anisotropic porous media.
  • Magnetohydrodynamic instability patterns in complex channel flow systems.
  • Odd-viscosity induced instability of fluid flow over vibrating inclined surfaces.

Publications

  • M. Saha and S. Ghosh, “On the magnetohydrodynamic instability pattern of an anisotropic porous media flow in a rotating channel,” Physics of Fluids, 38(1), 014122 (2026).
  • M. M. Hossain, M. Saha, S. Ghosh, and H. Behera, “Stability dynamics of an odd viscosity induced fluid flow over a vibrating inclined surface,” Physics of Fluids, 37(10), 104108 (2025).
  • M. Saha, S. Sengupta, S. Mukhopadhyay, and S. Ghosh, “Flow instability in a rotating channel loaded with an anisotropic porous material,” Computers & Fluids, 299, 106689 (2025).
