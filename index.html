About Me

I am a researcher working in computational fluid dynamics and hydrodynamic stability. My research focuses on instability mechanisms in complex flow systems including rotating channel flows, porous media flows, and magnetohydrodynamic systems using spectral numerical methods and high-performance computing.

Ongoing Research

  • Non-modal stability analysis and transient growth mechanisms in rotating channel flows.
  • Instability and transition mechanisms in porous media flows with anisotropic permeability.
  • Spectral numerical methods for hydrodynamic stability analysis of complex fluid systems.

Publications
